What These Metrics Mean

Reporting Time Eliminated — Each flagged shipment is one you don't need to manually research. At ~3 minutes per exception, the platform saves real operator time every day.

Delay Detection Speed — The platform evaluates every shipment on load. Pre-pilot, delays were caught manually, often 48+ hours late. Now they surface immediately.

Status Questions Reduced — Every visible exception on the dashboard is an email or Teams message that doesn't need to be sent. Self-service visibility replaces reactive communication.

Exception Coverage — 100% of loaded shipments are evaluated against configurable thresholds. No manual spot-checking, no gaps.

Pilot Tracking Approach

These metrics are designed to be reviewed weekly during the pilot period. Track them in a simple spreadsheet alongside:

  • Number of vendor follow-ups triggered by the platform
  • Corrections to exception flags (false positives)
  • User feedback from operators
  • Threshold adjustments made and why

The goal is not perfect data on day one. The goal is: "Is this better than what we had yesterday?"
